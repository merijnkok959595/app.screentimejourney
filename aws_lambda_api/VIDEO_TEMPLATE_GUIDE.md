## Overview

This guide explains how to create compelling milestone video templates for the Screen Time Journey video generation system.

## ğŸ“ Template Specifications

### Required Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 video codec, AAC audio codec)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 (9:16 aspect ratio - vertical/portrait)
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds (15s recommended for quick social sharing)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Bitrate**: 5-10 Mbps (good quality without being too large)
- **Audio**: Background music, royalty-free, moderate volume

### Why These Specs?

- **1080x1920**: Optimized for mobile viewing (Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels)
- **15 seconds**: Perfect length for social media, keeps viewers engaged
- **Vertical**: Most users view on phones in portrait mode
- **MP4/H.264**: Universal compatibility across all devices

## ğŸ¬ Creating Your Template

### Option 1: Using Stock Footage + Music

**Tools Needed:**
- Video editor (DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, CapCut)
- Stock footage site (Pexels, Pixabay, Artgrid)
- Royalty-free music (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, YouTube Audio Library)

**Steps:**

1. **Find Background Footage** (1080x1920)
   - Search terms: "motivational", "success", "achievement", "journey", "meditation"
   - Look for: nature, sunrise/sunset, mountains, ocean waves, abstract motion
   - Ensure: No existing text/watermarks in areas where overlays will appear

2. **Select Background Music**
   - Genre: Uplifting, inspirational, calm
   - Length: At least 15 seconds
   - Energy: Medium-high energy but not overwhelming
   - Example tracks: "Motivational Journey", "Success Stories", "Rise Up"

3. **Edit in Video Editor**

   **Timeline:**
   ```
   0-3s:   Calm intro (gradual reveal)
   3-6s:   Build up energy
   6-12s:  Peak energy/main content
   12-15s: Gentle outro/fade
   ```

   **Text Areas to Keep Clear:**
   ```
   Top (200px):        Greeting area ("Hi Merijn!")
   Center (400px):     Main achievement (Level display)
   Bottom Left (650px): Days counter
   Bottom Right (650px): Global rank
   Bottom Center (800px): Next level CTA
   ```

4. **Export Settings**
   ```
   Format: H.264 (MP4)
   Resolution: 1080x1920
   Frame Rate: 30fps
   Bitrate: 8 Mbps (VBR, 2-pass)
   Audio: AAC, 192 kbps, stereo
   ```

### Option 2: Using Motion Graphics Software

**Tools:** After Effects, Motion

**Process:**
1. Create 1080x1920 composition
2. Design animated background:
   - Subtle particle effects
   - Gradient animations
   - Shape morphing
   - Color transitions
3. Add audio track
4. Export with text areas clear

### Option 3: Using AI Video Generators

**Tools:** Runway ML, Pika Labs, Synthesia

**Process:**
1. Generate background with prompt: "Inspirational journey background, abstract, motivational, vertical video"
2. Ensure 1080x1920 output
3. Add royalty-free music
4. Export as MP4

## ğŸ¨ Design Best Practices

### Visual Guidelines

1. **Background**
   - Not too busy (text must be readable over it)
   - Consistent motion (not jerky or distracting)
   - Professional quality
   - Brand colors if possible (purple theme for STJ)

2. **Color Palette**
   - Use STJ brand colors:
     - Primary: #2E0456 (Deep purple)
     - Accent: #4a1875 (Light purple)
     - Background: Complementary colors (blue, gold, white)
   - Ensure good contrast for text overlays
   - Consider using color grading to match brand

3. **Motion**
   - Smooth, flowing motion
   - Not too fast (causes compression artifacts)
   - Not static (boring)
   - Purposeful camera moves (pan, zoom, dolly)

4. **Composition**
   - Keep focal point in center
   - Use rule of thirds
   - Leave breathing room for text
   - Consider safe zones (avoid edges)

### Audio Guidelines

1. **Music Selection**
   - Matches the mood of achievement
   - Not too loud (should complement, not dominate)
   - Clear, high-quality audio
   - No vocals (or minimal vocals that don't clash with text)

2. **Audio Levels**
   - Peak at -6dB to -3dB
   - No clipping or distortion
   - Gentle fade in/out at start/end
   - Consistent volume throughout

## ğŸ“Š File Size Optimization

Keep templates under 50MB for faster processing:

1. **Use H.264 codec** (not H.265/HEVC)
2. **Limit bitrate** to 5-8 Mbps
3. **Use 2-pass encoding** for better compression
4. **Reduce audio bitrate** to 128-192 kbps if needed
5. **Avoid excessive grain** or noise (hard to compress)
